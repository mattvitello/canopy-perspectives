Why Us?

We're re-envisioning the approach to tree care by combining dendrocentric and community expectations. We commit to balancing the intersection between humankind and trees to create a safe, beneficial space for everyone.

About

Dendrocentrism, we define, as an outlook on the world with the point of view centered around tree species as a living, valuable entity. Although it is impossible to take away our human lens that we perceive and personify the world around us, Canopy Perspectives Tree Care (CPTC) attempts to buffer our biases with science.

Tree Care

We believe that all humans deserve access to a greenspace on the basis of health and safety. Over time when there is a tree in these spaces, maintenance is often imperative. Our team works hard to provide tree services no matter a client's neighborhood, income, or opportunity. We want to work with you to make your greenspace a safe part of your lifestyle.

Science

Scientific research about our ecological communities (trees, us, fungus, plants, insects.. etc) brings category to a world that does not desire order. Although it can only help us to learn, and better intentionally interact with the natural world, we hope to be able to choose the responsible options for both humans and trees.  Through attending seminars, webinars, and reading endless journal articles that apply to tree physiology, and urban trees we continue to grow our passion for the work of arboriculture.

If you desire creating a greenspace or notice your tree might pose a risk to your wellbeing contact us! we will work with you to develop a solution that fits your needs.
